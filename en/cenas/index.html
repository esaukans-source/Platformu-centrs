Pricing for contractors in Europe

Pick the plan based on your workload and growth goals.

Basic - 29 EUR/month

Best for: contractors who want steady leads with lower monthly costs.

  • Professional profile + portfolio + reviews
  • Up to 10 requests per month (then pay per lead)
  • Client chat
  • 1 work zone (city/district)

Pro - 79 EUR/month

Best for: contractors who want higher volume and visibility.

  • Unlimited requests
  • Higher priority in ranking and search
  • 2-3 work zones
  • Fast replies + automatic notifications

24/7 Emergency - 149 EUR/month

Best for: specialists accepting urgent jobs.

  • Access to emergency requests 24/7
  • Priority for urgent jobs
  • SLA: response within 5 minutes
  • Click-to-call + SMS alerts

Lead pricing (pay-per-lead or Basic overage)

  • Plumbing / electrical (emergency): 6-12 EUR per lead
  • Heating (boilers, heat pumps): 10-25 EUR per lead
  • Finishing / paving / foundations: 15-35 EUR per lead

What is a lead: the ability to unlock the customer contact details and start communication.

Lead quality guarantee: if the customer does not respond within 24h or contact details are invalid, we return 1 lead credit.
