# Komisiju Tabula

## Formula
- Klienta cena = gala summa, ko samaksa klients
- Platformas komisija = procents no klienta cenas
- Partnera bruto ienakums = klienta cena - platformas komisija - tiesas izpildes izmaksas

## Komisijas pec plana
| Plans   | Komisija no darba |
|---------|--------------------|
| Starter | 12%                |
| Growth  | 9%                 |
| Scale   | 7%                 |

## Piemers (1 darbs, klienta cena 1000 EUR, izpildes izmaksas 620 EUR)
| Plans   | Platformai | Partnerim bruto |
|---------|------------|-----------------|
| Starter | 120 EUR    | 260 EUR         |
| Growth  | 90 EUR     | 290 EUR         |
| Scale   | 70 EUR     | 310 EUR         |

## Menesha piemers (12 darbi pa 1000 EUR)
| Plans   | Platformas ienakums | Partnera bruto ienakums |
|---------|----------------------|-------------------------|
| Starter | 1440 EUR             | 3120 EUR                |
| Growth  | 1080 EUR             | 3480 EUR                |

Piezime:

- Partnera neto pe lna tiek aprekinata pec nodokliem un operativajam izmaksam.
